Forsaken Quest. A new startup shining in the light of the gaming industry and the meta-verse.

As we are entering a new era in the gaming industry involving AAA games and Virtual Reality. Forsaken Quest has been founded for the purpose of providing high quality immersive gaming experience both on consoles and inside your own reality.

“Only when we aren't scared of the big "what if?" and embrace the moonshot thinking required to work in VR will we be able to help move this industry forward to its full potential.”

This era has been taken over by the meta-verse and Virtual Reality gaming and that's what we aim to participate in providing full potential to the industry with the most enjoyable experience.

Our Future Plans

Forsaken Quest aims to develop FPS and RPG immersive games with fascinating SCI-FI stories that takes place in the near future. We focus on the open world experience where it gives you the feeling of living the game for yourself in and our of the Virtual world. Be prepared to enjoy high quality cross-play games on all platforms and join the competition and events of Forsaken Quest!
