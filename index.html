About Us

What We do?

Forsaken Quest is a tech startup that focuses on developing games for all platforms and AR/VR content. The startup was founded as of 2021 by the CEO Omar Shokry.

More About Us!

Gamification Services

In our Gamification Services, we combine the deep knowledge of the gaming industry into a product to succeed in your business.

Unreal Development

Our skilled Unreal game developers will assist you in developing tough and engaging levels and mechanics, as well as unique AR/VR/MR-based solutions for achieving diverse business objectives.

Game Testing

We are equally focused on QA testing games for many platforms. Put your project in the hands of a staff that will ensure the greatest level of game quality and player pleasure.

Game Porting

We provide game porting services from PC to mobile and consoles, as well as technological porting from one engine to another, with complete assistance from beginning to end.
